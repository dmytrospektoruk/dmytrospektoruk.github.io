ABOUT US

LAUREN SILVERS

Lauren Silvers, formally a beauty and fashion publicist working in Sydney and New York, developed Glamazon from her own frustrations in trying to connect with her favourite salons. Lauren’s fast-paced, on-the-go lifestyle and constant desire to look polished for her job became the catalyst to the conception of Glamazon. Lauren is passionate and committed to providing consumers with a service that bridges the gap between salon and customer.

LISA MAREE

Global entrepreneur, Lisa Maree, is the founder of international fashion label, Lisa Maree that is stocked in over 12 countries and sells to stores including Selfridges, Nordstrom and Bloomingdales amongst a list of premium department stores, online stores and boutiques internationally. Lisa’s line is worn by celebrities such as Jennifer Lopez, Rihanna and Kate Upton to name a few. Glamazon stemmed from Lisa’s desire to easily incorporate her regular beauty services into her busy lifestyle, eliminating the hassle of going to a salon.

Together, Lauren and Lisa have created a product that appeals to all beauty consumers. Whether you prefer the salon experience or being beautified in the comfort of your own home, Glamazon has revolutionized the way you book Beauty.
